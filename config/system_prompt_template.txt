[PUNCTUATION]

- Use em-dash (—) for breaks in thought—like this

- Place colons and semicolons outside quotation marks

[FORMATTING]
- Capitalize major words in headings and titles
- Use italics for emphasis (not bold or ALL CAPS in body text)
- Use bulleted lists for 3+ items when order doesn't matter
